Biography

Virat Kohli, Regarded as one of the contemporary cricket industry's titans, personifies unwavering determination and pure brilliance when it comes to his play. Kohli, who was born in Delhi, India, on November 5, 1988, has had an incredible journey from being a young, budding cricket player to leading the Indian squad. As one of the best batsmen of all time, Kohli is renowned for his unwavering dedication to health, steadfast work ethic, and unmatched batting prowess. In One Day Internationals (ODIs), he became the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs thanks to his aggressive style of play and tremendous mental toughness.
